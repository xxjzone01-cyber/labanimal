Refund Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

1. 30-Day Refund Guarantee

We offer a 30-day unconditional refund guarantee for all paid subscriptions. If you are not satisfied with the Service within 30 days of your initial subscription, you may request a full refund.

2. Refund Conditions

2.1 Eligible for Refund

  • Request made within 30 days of initial subscription
  • Significant feature defect that cannot be resolved within a reasonable time
  • Service unusable due to our fault

2.2 Not Eligible for Refund

  • Request made after the 30-day refund period
  • More than 50% of paid feature quota already used
  • Account terminated due to Terms of Service violation
  • Free plan (Academic Free) does not involve refunds

3. Refund Process

To request a refund, follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to refund@labanimal.tech
  2. Please include the following in your email:
    • Your account email
    • Subscription plan name
    • Reason for refund (optional)
  3. We will review your refund request within 3 business days
  4. Once approved, the refund will be returned to your PayPal account within 5-10 business days

4. Canceling Subscription

You may cancel your subscription at any time:

  • Log in to the Subscription Management page
  • Click the "Cancel Subscription" button
  • Cancellation will take effect at the end of the current billing cycle
  • You may continue using the Service until the end of the cycle
  • Cancellation does not automatically trigger a refund

5. Downgrades and Upgrades

Upgrade: takes effect immediately, charged on a pro-rata basis for the remaining days.
Downgrade: takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle, no refund issued.

6. Special Circumstances

We will provide a full refund in the following cases:

  • Data loss caused by our system failure
  • Duplicate charges
  • Unauthorized charges

7. Contact

For any refund-related questions, please contact us:

  • Refund email: refund@labanimal.tech
  • Support email: support@labanimal.tech
  • GitHub Issues: Submit Issue
