Core Features Built for Compliance

Every feature maps directly to AAALAC certification and USDA inspection requirements — not a repurposed project management tool.

Dynamic Cage Density

Auto-calculates max housing density per NRC Guide 2011. Supports mice, rats, hamsters, guinea pigs, and rabbits. Auto-locks on overcapacity.

AVMA Euthanasia Validation

Real-time validation of euthanasia methods against AVMA Guidelines 2020. Auto-matches compliant methods by species, age, and equipment.

21 CFR Part 11 E-Signatures

WYSIWYG electronic signatures with SHA-256 hash chain audit trail. Meets FDA 21 CFR Part 11 compliance requirements.

IACUC Protocol Management

Full protocol lifecycle with automatic 3R validation (Replace/Reduce/Refine), USDA pain classification (D/E), and approval workflows.

Quarantine Block Mechanism

New animals automatically enter quarantine. Cannot be assigned to experiment cages until released, preventing cross-contamination.

Compliance Report Signing

PDF reports embed RSA-signed QR codes for authenticity verification. Supports online verification at labanimal.tech/verify.
